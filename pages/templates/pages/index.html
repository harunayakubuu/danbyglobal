About Us

DANBY Global Nigeria is a waste management company that deals in the business of collection, processing, and exporting of PLASTICS WASTE such as PET (polyethylene terephthalate) plastic, (Film Bottle, PE, PP, HDPE, BOPP, LLDPE, LDPE, and any other polybag waste), ABS plastic, Expanded Polystyrene (EPS), Polycarbonate PC, polyvinyl chloride (PVC) and any other related engineered plastic waste.
We articulate effectively the different knowledge areas of Plastic and Non-Plastic waste management, which include; collecting/purchasing, shredding, exporting, and recycling processes.
We export to Europe, China, India, Hong Kong, Malaysia, Thailand, and Korea.
